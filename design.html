Website Style Guide

 

In this section i will be discussing how, in light of the UX and UI theories discussed in class the wireframe of my website has changed. My wireframe has changed many times with many different iterations, the image above is one of the first wireframes made for this website.

 

In the theories discussed the Van Restoff Effect is discussed, i have made use of this effect in the menu header of my website by making the about and design elements of the menu seporate from the main home page and two blog pages. this is because van Restoffs effect states that when similar objects are present the one that differs the most is the most memorable, i have used this to make my home page and blog pages memorable.

 

The centralised text on the nav bar draws attention to the centre of the page, the pages have been constructed in such a way that they are very simple and easy to use, all of the information that one requires from this site is easily found and accessed.
