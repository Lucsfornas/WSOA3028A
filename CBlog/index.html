My Characters description and rationale

 

My character is a thrill and adventure seeker who is always looking to experience something new that he hasn't seen before. Being highly extroverted he finds comfort in crowds and being with people sharing his experiences. With being highly extroverted he is also quite hot headed and impatient at times when he is frustrated or uneasy but he is also one of the most laid back and easy going people when he is comfortable in his enviroment. Being based in the city of Cape Town, South Africa my character Dylan loves to travel and explore different people and cultures. He also has a keen eye for art and images when on adventures end exploring. He has quite a broad sense of humor anything from well placed comments to funny pictures makes him laugh, with this sense of humor being so prominant he often jokes around a lot and tires to make light of dim situations.
