Characters description and rationale

 

My character is a thrill and adventure seeker who is always looking to experience something new that he hasn't seen before. Being highly extroverted he finds comfort in crowds and being with people sharing his experiences. With being highly extroverted he is also quite hot headed and impatient at times when he is frustrated or uneasy but he is also one of the most laid back and easy going people when he is comfortable in his enviroment. Being based in the city of Johannesburg, South Africa my character Dylan loves to travel and explore different people, cultures and places.

 

He also has a keen eye for art and images when on adventures end exploring leading to him collecting memes and comedic images that he finds funny. He has quite a broad sense of humor anything from well placed comments, to funny pictures makes him laugh, with this sense of humor being so prominant he often jokes around a lot and tires to make light of dim situations. my character is also very OCD about preperation before going out on an adventure, checking the weather forecast dayly before going out to make sure that he is dressed appropriately, because theres nothing worse than a cold breeze when you are wearing shorts out. He is very rational and rarely allows his emotions affect his desisions. This prodominant rational thinking is very useful in stressful situations allowing him to make the best logical desision that he can make.
